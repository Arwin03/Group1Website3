Campaign For Tobacco-Free!


Problem About Us Get Involved

Ninety percent of adult smokers started this deadly habit when they were teenagers or younger. Youth therefore have a crucial role and strong voice in the battle against tobacco. They push elected officials to act, encourage their peers to abstain from tobacco use, and oppose the tobacco industry and its dishonest advertising. The Campaign for Tobacco-Free Kids supports young leaders who are working to rid the next generation of tobacco use through our Youth Initiatives.
